My intuition completely failed me, I failed to grasp even the slightest idea on what it could possibly mean.  But now, a couple of years later, I'd say I've matured more. I sat back down and then I tried to understand what Descartes was trying to say. I had a very fruitful and enlightening chat with Gemini on this and once the notion became clear, I was hit with several realizations. 

First, before we establish what this phrase means. ***Cogito, ergo sum***, we need to take a moment and understand who Descartes was and how did he come about giving the world such a foundational and influential statement. 

**Cogito**, meaning I think (or I am thinking to be more linguistically accurate) and **ergo sum**, meaning therefore I am. No new revelations here.
