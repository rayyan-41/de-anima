#personal
Often times I find myself in wonder and in thought. 
This is where I write about what I think about.
- - -
 ##### Note:
See, I realized, early on in my years, that I thoroughly enjoy philosophy. I find wisdom in it. Today's time is defined by materialism and mass consumerism. You have so many people that **live only for the world.** Vanity, vanity and vanity. Naturally, it comes as no surprise that one may find this way of living deeply unfulfilling. Why must we confine our purpose of life into containers of mindless, artificial ecstasies. 
 
This is what I realized early on and it made sense to me to think about the world in a grander scale. You need only look at the most influential people in history, the big minds, the revolutionaries of intellect and self-fulfillment to swiftly deduce that they are unique and superior to the common man. They are gifted by God. It's hard to translate my passion for these kinds of topics into words; they cannot carry the same burst of energy. But I can note down my thoughts and what I think about the world and life itself, so that it can be documented for the future, to let my kids discover these notes and instill within themselves and their kids, devotion to knowledge, intellect and God.
