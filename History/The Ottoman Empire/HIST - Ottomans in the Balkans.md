### HIST - The Ottomans in the Balkans
The expansion of the Ottoman Empire into the Balkans during the 14th and 15th centuries transformed the political landscape of Southeast Europe. Following the Battle of Kosovo in 1389 and the fall of the Second Bulgarian Empire, the Ottomans established a firm foothold that would last for centuries.
