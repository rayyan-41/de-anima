# History - Map of Contents

> *"History is a clock that people use to tell their political and cultural time of day." — John Henrik Clarke*

## Domain Overview

The History domain is the chronicler of human civilization. It tracks the rise and fall of empires, the lives of influential personalities, and the defining battles and movements that have shaped the world. The vault currently emphasizes the Islamic Golden Age, the Holy Roman Empire, and the Ottoman Empire.
