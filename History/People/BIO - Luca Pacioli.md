# BIO - Luca Pacioli (1447–1517)

### Inception: The Franciscan Friar of Mathematics
Fra Luca Bartolomeo de Pacioli was an Italian mathematician, Franciscan friar, and collaborator with Leonardo da Vinci.

### Peak: The Summa and Double-Entry Bookkeeping
Pacioli is best known as the "Father of Accounting and Bookkeeping" because he was the first person to publish a work on the double-entry system of book-keeping on the continent.
