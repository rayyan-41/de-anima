This is a master list on some of the most notable scholars that lived and thrived during the Golden Age of Islam . I am making this list for several purposes:
1) To ensure that their work is acknowledged by people like me: history enthusiasts and Muslims that want to recognize and appreciate the years upon years of research and development that our scholars before us gave the world which, in all honesty, gets critically under-represented and upheld in contrast to the more contemporary, mostly Latin and European scholars. 
2) To motivate myself and the Muslim world to study these people and strive to be like them, both in the fields of science, as well as piety and devotion to religion and the right cause.
