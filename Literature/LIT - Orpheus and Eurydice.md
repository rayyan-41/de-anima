### LIT - Orpheus and Eurydice

The tragic myth of the master musician who descended into the Underworld to reclaim his wife.

**Primary Source:** Ovid's [[Ovid's-Metamorphosis.pdf|Metamorphosis]].

- - -
The story of Orpheus and Eurydice is found in many preserved old texts, but Ovid's account remains the most definitive for the Western literary canon.
